BIO

CAKE is an alternative rock band from Sacramento, CA. The band was formed in 1991. They are known for their wide range of musical styles including country, rock, funk, Iranian folk music, Mariachi, and hip hop.

Cake incorporates a wide range of genres into its music, including country music, mariachi, new wave, college rock, jazz, funk, Iranian folk music, Brazilian music and hip hop. The band is most often noted for three things: the prominence of DiFiore's trumpet lines, McCrea's ironic, sarcastic lyrics, and his monotone vocals.
